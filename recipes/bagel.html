Egg McMuffin

Ingredients

  • 1 Egg
  • 1 Round Egg Cast Iron Pan
  • 1 Bagel
  • Pinch Salt & Papricka
  • 1 Slice Cheese
  • Bacon (optional)
  1. Crack egg into egg pan on medium heat. Season with salt and papricka
  2. Put bagel in toster
  3. Add cheese to egg in pan
  4. Get a plate, place toasted bagel on plate and place the ROUND egg on that bad boy.
    Cover in a dalup of hot sauce and stuff your face
