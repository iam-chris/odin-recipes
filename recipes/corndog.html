Corndogs

Ingredients

  • 10 frankfurter
  • 1 cup flour
  • 1 egg
  • 1/2 cup of milk
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • Oil
  1. At first, impaling the frankfurters with skewer sticks.
  2. Then, prepare the tempura. In a bowl put the flour, egg, a teaspoon of salt, the baking powder and milk. The exact amount of milk varies because, depending on the size egg youâ€™ve used and the brand flour because not all flours absorb the same amount of liquid. Beat well with a fork. There must not be lumps in the batter. Finally it has to be a certain thickness but rather fluid
  3. After that, put the batter in a tall glass or other tall container.
  4. Now, roll the frankfurters in the glass and impregnate of the batter.
  5. Finally, remove the excess batter and fry. This dough is very delicate so the heat must be high but not too much or they burn. Once fried, let rest on paper towels before eating.
