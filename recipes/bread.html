Bread

Ingredients

  • 3 Cups Flour
  • 1 3/4 Cup Warm Water
  • 1 Egg
  • 1 Yeast Pack
  • Pinch Salt
  • Pinch Sugar
  1. Add flour, salt, sugar, egg, yeast and warm water into a big bowl. Stir until a sticky ball.
  2. Add a little flour to dough and kneed it until smooth. Gently oil bowl, cover with towel and let rise 1 hour.
  3. Punch down dough and kneed until smooth.
  4. Flour counter and roll out dough into 12" square
  5. Roll your squre into a tube and place in oiled baking pan. Bake 35 minutes at 350 degrees freedom units
